Introduction

Klio AI is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information when you use our AI learning companion service.

Information We Collect and Store

  • Child's first name and age
  • Parent/guardian email address
  • Brief learning summaries (limited to 5 most recent conversations)
  • Child's interests and learning preferences (parent-controlled)

Data Retention and Deletion

We maintain strict data retention policies:

  • Chat conversations are automatically deleted after generating parent summaries
  • Only the 5 most recent conversation summaries are stored
  • Older summaries are automatically deleted
  • Parents have full control to delete any stored interests or preferences at any time

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Personalize your child's learning experience
  • Provide parents with brief activity summaries
  • Maintain parent-approved learning preferences
  • Ensure compliance with our safety guidelines

Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your family's information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all conversations
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Automatic deletion of conversation data
